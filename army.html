Infantry

Infantry battalions are the frontline soliders that do the fighting.

Mobile

Mobile battalions are fast moving units that are used for more specialized purposes. They are more expensive than Infantry as a result due to their mechanized nature.

Armored

Armored battalions use heavily armed and armored vehicles, like tanks, and are very expensive. However, the have great stats and are very useful.

Combat Support Battalions

These battalions are mainly made up of heavy equipment that are used to support other battalions in combat.

Mobile Combat Support Battalions

Fufills the same purpose of combat support battalions, but are more mobile.

Support Companies

Support companies provide support the entire division in combat by providing bonus stats.
