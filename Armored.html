Light tanks are very fast and manuvorable but are have thin armor and lower firepower.

Medium tanks are larger, have thicker armor, and have more firepower than light tanks, making them more suited for manuvore warfare and tank on tank engagments. They are however more expensive than light tanks.

Heavy tanks are meant for breaking through enemy line and creating gaps for other units to exploit. For this reason they are heavily armed and armored, which makes them very expensive.

Modern tanks are the pinicle of tank development by combining the speed of medium tanks with the firepower and armor protection of heavy tanks. They are however expensive to produce and take a long time to research.

Amphibious tanks are tanks that support marines in amphibious landings and thus have amphibious capabilites.

Light amphibious tanks are light tanks that are modified for amphibious landings.

Medium amphibious tanks are medium tanks that are modified for amphibious landings.

Heavy amphibious tanks are heavy tanks that are modified for amphibious landings.

Light tank destroyers are light tanks modified with a medium caliber gun by removing the turret.

Medium tank destroyers are medium tanks modified with a heavy caliber gun by removing the turret.

Heavy tank destroyers are heavy tanks modified with a larger gun by removing the turret.

Modern tank destroyers are modern tanks modified with a larger gun by removing the turret.

Light self propelled artillery are light tanks modified to carry light artillery

Medium self propelled artillery are medium tanks modified to carry medium artillery

Heavy self propelled artillery are heavy tanks modified to carry heavy artillery

Modern self propelled artillery are modern tanks modified to carry modern artillery

Light self propelled anti-air are light tanks modified to carry light anti-air

Medium self propelled anti-air are medium tanks modified to carry medium anti-air

Heavy self propelled anti-air are heavy tanks modified to carry heavy anti-air

Modern self propelled anti-air are modern tanks modified to carry modern anti-air
