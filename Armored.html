Light tanks are very fast and manuvorable but are have thin armor and lower firepower.

Medium tanks are larger, have thicker armor, and have more firepower than light tanks, making them more suited for manuvore warfare and tank on tank engagments. They are however more expensive than light tanks.

Heavy tanks are meant for breaking through enemy line and creating gaps for other units to exploit. For this reason they are heavily armed and armored, which makes them very expensive.

Modern tanks are the pinicle of tank development by combining the speed of medium tanks with the firepower and armor protection of heavy tanks. They are however expensive to produce and take a long time to research.
