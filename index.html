Overview

Hearts of Iron IV is a grand strategy game that allows you to take control on any nations in 1936 or 1939 and control it to recreate World War 2 or reshape history to your liking. This game was created by Paradox Development Studio and published by Paradox Interactive. It features both single player and multiplayer modes. The main focus is in the military, which consists of the Army, Navy, and Airforce. All three of these branches of the military are crucial in helping nations win wars against other, so it is important for the player to understand all three to efficiently utilize them.

Army

The Army wages the land battles to secure territory and defend against attacks from other armies.

Navy

The Navy maintains control over the oceans and seas to help protect convoys delivering supplies or resources to your nation. It also helps to contest other nation's navies from gaining naval superiority.

Air Force

The Air Force provides support for any battle, whether by being on the oceans or on land. They maintain control of the skies to help protect your armies or fleets from attack by other nation's Air Force, and provide support to your armies or fleets when needed.
